Icons and Logos

(drawn with SVG)




Objective: Recreate a bunch of company icons with as much precision as possible.

Problem: How can we easily make icons that have some amount of symmetry?

Solution: We can make only certain parts of the logo, then use to duplicate and rotate it the rest of the way around. This makes the process of creating these logos super fast.
