Apply

All you need to know about applying!


Requirements

Applying can seem like a daunting task. You never really know what they're looking for, which is why we've tried to simplifiy this as much as possible! As long as you are able to demonstrate skill in your area of application you're good to go!

When?

You can ALWAYS apply to Team Blocksanity! We're open application which means we will always be looking for new members and you can try and apply as many times as you want!

How?

Easily apply by clicking the link above and filling out the form! As soon as your application is reviewed we'll hit you up!
